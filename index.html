Email LinkedIn Google Scholar GitHub

About

I work on wireless systems for low-power sensing and communication. My research explores how ambient signals can be used to enable new sensing and communication capabilities under extreme power constraints.

PhD Advisor: Zerina Kapetanovic

Current Research

My current work includes communication and sensing using thermal noise, as well as backscatter communication using synthetic aperture radar satellites.
